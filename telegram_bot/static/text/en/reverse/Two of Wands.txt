In a general context, the Two of Wands reversed represents indecisiveness, fear of change and fear of the unknown. When reversed, it is a Minor Arcana card of lack of planning, restricted options, being held back and disappointment. It can represent staying put, deciding not to go, cancelled or delayed travel or cancelled emigration. The Two of Wands reversed can also signify choosing the safest bet, choosing a mundane life, anti-climax and self-doubt. It can also signify the sudden arrival or return of a person into your life.
