In a general context, the Ace of Swords reversed can represent a lack of ideas, misinformation, confusion and failure. It can also indicate intellectual inability, lack of mental clarity, memory loss and an inability to concentrate. This Minor Arcana card reversed can signify a lack of communication, creative blocks, lack of vision and frustration. It represents making wrong decisions, injustice and a lack of assertiveness. It can also indicate bad news in relation to legal matters or legal contracts or legal letters that may not be favourable to you.
