In a love Tarot spread, if you are in a relationship the Four of Wands is a great omen representing happy family life, success, stability, security and laying down roots so your relationship should be going very well when it appears. It is also a Minor Arcana card signifying celebrations and as such, can represent parties, welcoming ceremonies, baptisms and family coming together. It is a very strong indicator of a wedding so if you are hoping to tie the knot it is a very welcome card to see. If you are single, the Four of Wands is also a good omen indicating parties, celebrations and feeling like you fit in so you should be enjoying single life when it appears. It can also represent reunion so a past love may come back into your life when it appears.
