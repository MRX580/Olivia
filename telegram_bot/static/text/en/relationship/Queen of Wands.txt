In a love Tarot reading, if you are in a relationship the Queen of Wands can indicate that you are in a relationship with someone who embodies the qualities outlined in the above section or you are displaying some of those characteristics yourself in your love life. Remember, she can be fiery but life with her is never boring. This Minor Arcana card can also indicate that you and your partner may feel a surge in energy and optimism within the relationship. Things should be going very well and you both should be feeling very confident, loved and excited. If you are planning a baby, this card is one of the best omens you can get as it represents motherhood and fertility! If you are single, the Queen of Wands can indicate that you will meet someone who embodies the qualities outlined in the above section or you are displaying some of those characteristics yourself in your love life. This card can also indicate that you are ready to go out and meet someone new. You have the right balance of independence, fun and sexiness. Be confident in yourself! People will be attracted to you!
